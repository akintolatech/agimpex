About us

"AG IMPEX" LLC was founded in 2018 with one clear goal: to become a reliable and long-term partner in the construction sector in Armenia. For more than 7 years, we have been providing the Armenian market with high-quality, certified construction goods at competitive prices through both import and wholesale/retail sales, satisfying the requirements of both large construction companies and individual customers. Starting from 2026, "AG IMPEX" LLC is also entering the market with two new brands: Metals Holding and Prime Stone.
